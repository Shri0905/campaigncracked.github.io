The Problem with Marketing Analysis

Most campaign breakdowns stop at "creative execution" or "virality metrics." They celebrate the what without questioning the why.

Campaign Cracked goes deeper. Every case study here dissects the consumer insight, brand positioning, and strategic intent behind Indian campaigns—then asks the uncomfortable question: Did this actually solve a business problem?

What You'll Find Here

This isn't about whether an ad looked good or went viral. It's about whether the strategy behind it was sound.

Each analysis applies frameworks from brand strategy, consumer psychology, and competitive positioning to evaluate:

  • What consumer truth the campaign identified (or missed)
  • Whether the brand's positioning justified the creative approach
  • What worked, what failed, and what alternatives existed

Real campaigns. Real frameworks. Zero fluff.
